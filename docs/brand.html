04 / Type

Two families, split by meaning.

Literata is the voice. The serif carries everything with meaning — the partner's words, your own words, the Career Brief, headlines, leads, body, and eyebrows. It reads like a letter, not a UI.

Hanken Grotesk is chrome only. The sans handles nav, buttons, field labels, badges, and metadata — nothing with meaning. Chosen over Inter/system-sans, which read generic.

07 / Hard rules

Never violate.

Gold is a dial, not a switch

Gold appears only when something is genuinely earned — a filled held line, a Story Score, a complete brief, a real credential — and it gilds by degree: the more earned, the fuller the foil, from step 1 (neutral, unearned) to step 4 (full arrival). Stage it on pine so it reads as gilt. Never for eyebrows, decoration, or wayfinding — being on a page is not an achievement, so the active-nav marker stays pine.

No AI-sparkle ornament

No neon/violet gradients, glow, circuit motifs, robot or spark mascots, or "AI-powered" copy. Capability is demonstrated, never announced.

Serif is the voice; sans is chrome

Literata carries everything with meaning; Hanken Grotesk handles chrome only. Nothing with meaning is set in the sans. No system-sans, no monospace.

No eyebrow that restates the headline

The headline stands alone. Use eyebrows sparingly and never as a section header — for structure use a real heading or a sans chrome label.

Restraint is the signal

Blank space and typographic confidence are the primary tools. Flat surfaces, warm hairlines, generous gaps. Decoration works against the trust this product must build.

No chat-widget register

The conversation reads as a skilled 1:1 interview — no message bubbles, typing dots, or playful avatars. Set dialogue in the display serif.

You hold the pen

It's your story — you hold the pen; the partner just helps you find the words. The user is the author and protagonist; the product draws out and re-threads their value and never writes over them. Copy keeps the user as the subject.

AI, named honestly — as empowerment

The career partners are AI, and we say so plainly — never a badge, never disguised as human. Most AI flattens everyone into the same average; a Meritous partner works from your story alone, so you sound more like yourself, not less.

Reflect, never direct

The partner reflects back and checks — here's what I'm hearing; does that sound right? It confirms what matters once and moves; it never quadruple-checks or asks permission for every step.

Customer-facing language only

Every word the user sees is warm, plain, editorial. No internal jargon, no spec-speak, and no external-validation overclaim — kill "Verified"; ground everything in the user's own words ("in your words," "confirmed by you").
