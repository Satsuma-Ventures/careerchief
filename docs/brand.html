07 · Hard rules

Never violate.

Discipline the accent

Brass appears only at genuine milestones — a completed brief, a breakthrough score, a real credential. Used freely, it reads as decoration and stops meaning anything.

No AI-sparkle ornament

No neon/violet gradients, glow, circuit motifs, robot or spark mascots, or "✨ AI-powered" copy. Capability is demonstrated, never announced.

Restraint is the signal

Blank space and typographic confidence are the primary tools. Decoration is the interface showing off — it works against the trust this product must build.

No chat-widget register

The conversation reads as a skilled 1:1 interview — no message bubbles, typing dots, or playful avatars. Set dialogue in the display serif.

Authority through type

Libertinus Serif carries the gravity — one family, hierarchy from size and weight. Not illustration, not color, not motion.

Not résumé-builder SaaS

No dashboard-of-widgets, ATS-score gauges everywhere, or template-picker energy. This product excavates; it does not reformat.
